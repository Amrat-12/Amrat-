About Our School

Govt:Campus High School Bhorilo is committed to providing a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning.

Founded in 1995, our school has grown to become one of the top-ranked institutions in the region, with a focus on STEM education, arts, and athletics.

Admission Terms and Conditions
Application Process

Submission of this application form does not guarantee admission to Greenwood High School. All applications are reviewed by our admissions committee.

Required Documents

After submitting this form, you will need to provide the following documents to complete your application:

  • Official transcripts from current school
  • Two teacher recommendations
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Immunization records
Assessment

All applicants are required to complete an admissions assessment. You will be contacted to schedule this after submitting your application.

Privacy Policy

All information provided in this application will be kept confidential and used solely for admissions purposes.
